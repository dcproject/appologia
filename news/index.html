  • August 15, 2012

    The Christian Research Institute (CRI) has published a series of videos that discusses their research into the ministry of Watchman Nee, Witness Lee, and the local churches. You can view them either on the CRI site or on a special YouTube playlist. The site also has links to free PDFs of a special edition of the “We Were Wrong” issue of its Christian Research Journal in several languages, including English.

    These are in addition to four videos CRI posted earlier featuring Hank Hanegraaff:

    • Discussing CRI’s findings concerning the ministry of Watchman Nee and Witness Lee;

    • Discussing CRI’s conclusions concerning the publications of Living Stream Ministry;

    • Speaking at the University of Southern California concerning the Christians on Campus organization; and

    • Talking about the impact of Watchman Nee as one of the two great figures in China’s religious history.
